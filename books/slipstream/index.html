Slipstream

An adult contemporary romance set inside elite motorsport, where ambition, class, and scrutiny turn attraction into something dangerous to ignore.

Rafe Vale has spent long enough on the edge of Formula One to know what the sport rewards and what it uses up. Leonie March comes from the other side of that world: a family name that opens doors, shapes deals, and expects loyalty long after feeling has stopped mattering.

As opportunity, attention, and pressure build across the season, what grows between them has to survive money, reputation, scrutiny, and the machinery of elite sport itself. Slipstream is tense, glamorous, and emotionally serious, asking what it costs to choose something true inside a world built to manage appearances.
