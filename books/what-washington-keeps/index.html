What Washington Keeps

Cities of Exile Book 2: a romance set against guarded histories, private loyalties, and the things a powerful city chooses not to say aloud.

What Washington Keeps continues the Cities of Exile series with a story of love, guarded history, and the private truths that survive inside public places.

In a city built on memory, ambition, and carefully managed surfaces, feeling becomes its own kind of risk. The novel follows the tension between what is protected, what is hidden, and what must finally be faced when intimacy presses against loyalty, reputation, and the past.
