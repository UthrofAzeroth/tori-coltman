The Last Bright Things · Book 5

Silver in the Rain

The Last Bright Things Book 5: a contemporary romance about weather, memory, and the fragile shine that remains after loss.

Silver in the Rain continues The Last Bright Things with a contemporary romance shaped by weather, memory, and the quiet aftermath of loss.

Atmospheric and emotionally restrained, the novel follows the glimmer of possibility that remains when life has been dark for too long. It is a love story about endurance, tenderness, and the unexpected brightness that can appear when people stop trying to outrun what hurt them.
