Nobody's Daughter

The Last Bright Things Book 4: a small-town novel of love, work, and coming home.

Nobody's Daughter is a small-town novel of love, work, and coming home, continuing The Last Bright Things with a story about return and repair.

When Tess comes back to Briar Point, the life she left behind is no longer safely in the past. Family obligation, work, memory, and unfinished feeling converge around a town where everyone knows enough to wound and enough to care. The result is a grounded romance about belonging, independence, and the difficult grace of coming home as someone changed.
