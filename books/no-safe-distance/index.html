No Safe Distance

A romantic suspense novel about trust, danger, and the pull between two people who cannot keep a safe distance from what they feel or what threatens them.

No Safe Distance is a romantic suspense novel about closeness under pressure, where trust becomes dangerous and distance is not enough to keep the past contained.

The story brings together emotional intensity and threat, following two people drawn toward each other while danger narrows the space around them. It is a love story shaped by fear, loyalty, attraction, and the hard choices that come when safety and feeling point in different directions.
