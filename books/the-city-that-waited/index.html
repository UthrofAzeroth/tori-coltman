The City That Waited

Cities of Exile Book 1: a contemporary romance about memory, return, and the city that holds a life in suspension until love makes movement possible.

The City That Waited opens the Cities of Exile series with a contemporary love story about memory, absence, and the pull of a place that has not finished with the people who left it.

Set around the emotional pressure of return, the novel explores what happens when longing, history, and present desire meet in a city that seems to have been holding its breath. It is a story about belonging, timing, and the possibility of beginning again without pretending the past has disappeared.
