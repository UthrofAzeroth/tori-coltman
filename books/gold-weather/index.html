The Last Bright Things · Book 6

Gold Weather

The Last Bright Things Book 6: a contemporary romance about warmth after difficulty, late light, and the courage to choose what still feels alive.

Gold Weather completes the current run of The Last Bright Things with a contemporary romance about warmth after difficulty and the late light that changes how a life looks.

The novel is shaped by recovery, tenderness, and the courage required to choose what still feels alive. It brings the series back to the emotional ground that defines it: love under pressure, hope without easy sentiment, and the bright things people keep carrying forward.
