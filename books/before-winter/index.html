Before Winter

A deeply intimate love story shaped by illness, care, time running short, and the emotional truth of what it means to stay close when everything changes.

Before Winter is rooted in closeness under pressure: practical care, shared time, and the quiet labour of love when ordinary routines are altered by illness. It is interested in grief, tenderness, endurance, and the emotional cost of time becoming suddenly precious.
