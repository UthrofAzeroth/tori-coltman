Book

Before Winter

An intimate love story about illness, care, and the emotional truth of staying close when time suddenly feels more fragile.

Before Winter stays close to the daily realities of care: practical tenderness, altered routines, and the emotional labour of love under pressure.

It is a novel about devotion, endurance, grief, and the way ordinary shared time can become suddenly precious when the future narrows and every small act of closeness starts to matter more.
