The Last Bright Things · Book 2

The Things We Tried to Save

The Last Bright Things Book 2: a novel of love, repair, and second chances.

The Things We Tried to Save continues The Last Bright Things with a contemporary romance about love, repair, and the fragile work of second chances.

The novel turns on what people keep, what they lose, and what they try to mend after life has already left its marks. Emotionally grounded and quietly hopeful, it follows the tension between damage and devotion, asking whether the things worth saving can survive being handled honestly at last.
