Cities of Exile · Book 5

Home After All

Cities of Exile Book 5: a contemporary romance about return, belonging, and the hard-won possibility of finding home after every road away.

Home After All brings the Cities of Exile sequence to its fifth book with a story of love, return, and the quiet reckoning that comes when the idea of home is no longer simple.

After distance, change, and the choices that once seemed necessary, the novel turns toward belonging: what can be rebuilt, what must be forgiven, and whether two people can make a future from the pieces of places they thought they had left behind.
