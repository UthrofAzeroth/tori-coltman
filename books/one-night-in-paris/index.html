One Night in Paris

Cities of Exile Book 3: a romance about one night, one city, and the kind of connection that changes the shape of everything after it.

One Night in Paris is a Cities of Exile romance about a brief encounter with consequences far larger than the time it occupies.

Paris becomes more than a backdrop: it is atmosphere, pressure, memory, and possibility. The novel follows the charged space between impulse and consequence, asking what can happen when one night forces two people to see themselves, and each other, more clearly than ordinary life allows.
