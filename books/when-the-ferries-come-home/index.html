Cities of Exile · Book 4

When the Ferries Come Home

Cities of Exile Book 4: a love story of crossings, return, old weather, and the emotional tide that brings people back to what still matters.

When the Ferries Come Home carries the Cities of Exile series into a story of crossings, return, and the quiet emotional force of coming back.

Built around the pull of coast, weather, memory, and unfinished feeling, the novel asks what home means after absence has changed everyone. It is a romance about distance, repair, and the moments when people finally stop moving away from what still matters.
