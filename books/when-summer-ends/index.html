When Summer Ends

A contemporary romance about timing, attraction, and the fragile intensity of connection when people meet outside the shape of ordinary life.

Set against a temporary season in which ordinary boundaries loosen, When Summer Ends is interested in attraction, timing, and the uncertain emotional weight of a connection that arrives outside the normal shape of life.
