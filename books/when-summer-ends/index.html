When Summer Ends

A contemporary romance about attraction, timing, and the intensity of connection when ordinary life is temporarily held at a distance.

When Summer Ends is drawn to the heightened feeling of temporary worlds: a suspended stretch of time, growing closeness, and the knowledge that ordinary life waits on the other side.

It is interested in longing, desire, hesitation, and the emotional risk of allowing something brief to matter deeply before the rest of life begins to pull both people back towards itself.
