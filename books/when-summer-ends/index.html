When Summer Ends

A contemporary romance about attraction, timing, and the intensity of connection when ordinary life is temporarily held at a distance.

When Summer Ends is a holiday romance about attraction, timing, and the heightened intimacy that can form when ordinary life is briefly held at a distance.

Warm, atmospheric, and emotionally grounded, it follows the pull between desire and reality as a temporary escape becomes something harder to leave behind. The result is a love story about summer intensity, honest feeling, and the difficult question of what remains when the season ends.
