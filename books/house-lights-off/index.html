The Last Bright Things · Book 3

House Lights Off

The Last Bright Things Book 3: a Los Angeles novel of love, work, and second chances.

House Lights Off is a Los Angeles romance about love, work, pressure, and the second chances people do not always believe they deserve.

Set in a city built on performance, ambition, and the hard edge between image and private life, the novel follows feeling after the lights go down. It is a story about the work people do for survival, the tenderness they try to hide, and the possibility of being seen without having to perform.
