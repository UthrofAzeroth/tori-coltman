Privacy note

This is a simple public author website. It currently exists to present author information, book pages, and links to external retailer pages.

The site does not presently offer reader accounts, contact forms, comments, or newsletter sign-ups, and it does not ask visitors to submit personal details through the site itself.

Book purchase links take visitors to external retailer pages. Once you leave this site, the retailer's own terms and privacy policies apply.

If the public setup changes later, this page should be updated so the live privacy note continues to reflect the site accurately.
