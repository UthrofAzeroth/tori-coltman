About the author

Emotionally grounded contemporary love stories.

Tori Coltman writes emotionally grounded contemporary love stories. Her fiction is drawn to ordinary settings, close emotional observation, and the moments when timing changes everything. Across her books, she returns to intimacy, longing, grief, hope, and the quiet ways people care for one another under pressure.
