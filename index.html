Tori Coltman

Contemporary love stories rooted in ordinary life, emotional truth, and the moments that change everything.

Tori Coltman writes fiction about connection, timing, hope, grief, and the quiet turns that alter a life. Her books stay close to emotional reality, following ordinary people through intimacy, hesitation, longing, care, and change.
