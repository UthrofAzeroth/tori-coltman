About the author

Love stories with warmth, restraint, and emotional honesty.

Tori Coltman writes contemporary fiction that stays close to emotional truth. Whether the setting is intimate and domestic, city-shaped, small-town, or touched by glamour, danger, power, and public scrutiny, the books return to tenderness, longing, hope, loss, and the practical acts by which people reveal what they feel.
